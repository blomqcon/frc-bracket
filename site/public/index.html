How to play?

The Game

  • Choose how you think each alliance will look at the end of alliance selection.

  • Two hours before alliance selection brackets are locked in and can no longer be modified.

Scoring

  • The bracket with the 'lowest score' wins.

  • There are 8 alliances each with 3 teams. This gives 24 team positions and oppertunties to earn points.

  • One point is added to a bracket for each overall pick away from each position.

    • If I put 2522 as the 3rd alliance captain but they wind up the 5th captain my bracket earns 2 points.
    • If I put 1778 as the 20th pick overall but they end up being the 8th captain my bracket earns 12 points.
    • If I put 4488 as the 2nd alliance captain but then the 1st alliance picks them as the 9th overall pick my bracket earns 7 points.
